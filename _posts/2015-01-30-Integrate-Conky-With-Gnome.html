Conky is not working well with Gnome? Try adding following lines to your ~/.conkyrc: 

1: own_window yes
2: own_window_type conky
3: own_window_transparent yes
4: own_window_hints undecorated,below,sticky,skip_taskbar,skip_pager
5: 
6: own_window_argb_visual yes
7: own_window_argb_value 255
